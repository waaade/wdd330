Local storage

To store something in javascript: localStorage.setItem(key, value)

To load something stored: localStorage.getItem(key)

Mobile Design

  • Design for mobile FIRST
  • Don't make users hunt for what they want
  • Remove unneccessary steps, make things quick
  • Reduce image use, use svg/webfonts
  • "Menu" with border (looks like button) got most clicks
  • Reduce "cognitive load"
  • Forms: multiple pages + progress bar. Autocomplete enabled.
  • Show passwords by default, but do give the option to hide
